I graduated from Univeristy of California, Santa Barbara with a Bachelor in Sociology and Education: Applied Psychology. I have worked in nonprofit for over 10 years - working mainly with unserved, at risk youth. My passion was empowering youth, especially females, to see beyond their perceived limits. Regardless of their socio-economic background, family structure, cultural or ethnic background, gender, age or success in traditional modalities, everyone has the potential to do what they want. Recently, I have decided to take the advice that I give to others: follow your passion.

When I was younger, I enjoyed playing with computers, especially DOS’s Aldo’s Adventure! I also loved customizing my myspace page! Somewhere between high school and college I lost it - it must have been Facebook *shakes her fist*

So now I am following a passion I had and I am woring towards becoming a Front End Developer. I am very excited for this new venture and I look forward to discovering this new world.
